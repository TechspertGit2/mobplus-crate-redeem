# mobplus-crate-redeem
Redeeming crate keys in Mobplus
## How to redeem
Please send me your Minecraft UUID (You can find it by pasting your username [here!](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Calculators/Player_UUID))
via email. My email address is techsperto@outlook.com. Send your UUID so I can check you have the redeemer item called "Type /crateredeem" in your inventory.
After you have done this, I will grant you your prize in-game!
